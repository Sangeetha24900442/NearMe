Hanuman Temple-Senneerkuppam

The Hanuman Temple located in Senneerkuppam, in the Tiruvallur district of Tamil Nadu, is a significant religious site dedicated to Lord Hanuman, a revered deity in Hinduism known for his strength, devotion, and loyalty to Lord Rama. This temple is popular among locals who visit to seek blessings for courage, strength, and protection in overcoming life's challenges.
